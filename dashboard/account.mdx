### Changing Password

For email/password accounts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Security">
    Click "Security" in account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Current Password">
    Verify your identity with your current password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Choose a strong new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Re-enter the new password and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Change password form">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/account-change-password.png" alt="Change Password" />
</Frame>

## Your Workspaces

Click a workspace to switch to it.

## Leaving a Workspace

To leave a workspace you don't own:

1. Find the workspace in Your Workspaces
2. Click "Leave Workspace"
3. Confirm

<Frame caption="Leave workspace confirmation">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/account-leave-workspace.png" alt="Leave Workspace" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
  You cannot leave a workspace if you're the only owner. Transfer ownership first or delete the workspace.
</Warning>

## Deleting Your Account

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent. All your data, including workspaces you own, will be deleted.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Account** → **Delete Account**
2. Enter your password to confirm
3. Acknowledge the consequences
4. Click "Delete Account"

<Frame caption="Delete account confirmation requiring password">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/account-delete-confirm.png" alt="Delete Account" />
</Frame>

Before deleting:
- Transfer ownership of any workspaces you want to preserve
- Download any files you need to keep
- Export templates you want to save
