## Upgrading Your Plan

### From the Dashboard

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the upgrade

Changes take effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle.

### What Happens When You Upgrade

- **Immediate access** to new limits and features
- **Prorated billing** for the current period
- **No data loss** - all templates and files preserved
- **API keys continue working** without changes

---

## Downgrading Your Plan

When downgrading:

- Change takes effect at the **end of your billing period**
- Ensure you're within new plan limits before the change
- Excess templates will become read-only (not deleted)
- Files beyond retention period will be queued for deletion

<Warning>
If you exceed the new plan's limits, some features may be restricted until you reduce usage.
</Warning>

## Exceeding Your Quota

When you reach your monthly PDF limit:

1. **API returns error** - `429 NO_CREDITS_AVAILABLE`
2. **Dashboard shows warning** - quota exceeded notification
3. **Options to continue:**
   - Purchase credit packs (instant)
   - Upgrade your plan
   - Wait for monthly reset

<Info>
Fileloom does not support automatic overages. This protects you from unexpected bills.
</Info>
