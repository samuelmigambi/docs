## Current Plan

The billing page shows your current subscription:

- **Plan name** — Free, Starter, Growth, Scale, etc.
- **Billing period** — Monthly or Annual
- **Next billing date** — When your subscription renews
- **Monthly quota** — PDFs included in your plan

<Frame caption="Current plan card with plan details and upgrade button">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/billing-current-plan.png" alt="Current Plan" />
</Frame>

## Changing Plans

### Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    From the billing page, click "Upgrade Plan".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Plan">
    Choose your new plan and billing period (monthly/annual).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter payment details through our secure checkout (Polar.sh).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant Activation">
    Your new plan limits are active immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Plan selection grid with all available plans">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/billing-plan-selector.png" alt="Plan Selector" />
</Frame>

<Info>
  Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly billing.
</Info>

<Frame caption="Checkout page powered by Polar.sh">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/billing-checkout.png" alt="Checkout Page" />
</Frame>

### Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period:

1. Click "Change Plan"
2. Select a lower tier
3. Confirm the change

Your current plan benefits continue until the period ends.

## Auto Top-Up

Enable automatic credit purchases when running low:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Auto Top-Up">
    Go to **Workspace Settings** → **Billing** → **Auto Top-Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Pack">
    Choose which credit pack to purchase automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Trigger">
    Configure when to trigger (e.g., when 90% of quota is used).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Limits">
    Optionally set a maximum number of auto-purchases per month.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Workspace Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select a reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

<Frame caption="Cancel subscription confirmation with reason selection">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/billing-cancel-confirm.png" alt="Cancel Subscription" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
  After cancellation, your workspace downgrades to Free at the end of the billing period. Templates and files remain but may be affected by lower limits.
</Warning>

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

1. Update your payment method in the billing portal
2. Retry the payment
3. Contact support if issues persist

<Frame caption="Failed payment notification with retry option">
  <img src="/images/dashboard/billing-payment-failed.png" alt="Payment Failed" />
</Frame>

After multiple failed payments, your subscription may be paused.
