Access account settings by clicking **Account** in the sidebar.

## Profile Settings

Update your personal information:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Display Name** | Your name shown in the dashboard |
| **Email** | Account email (used for login and notifications) |
| **Photo** | Profile picture (from Google/GitHub or uploaded) |

## Preferences

Customize your Fileloom experience:

### Theme

Choose your preferred appearance:

- **Light** — Light background
- **Dark** — Dark background
- **System** — Match your device settings

### Language

Select your preferred language for the dashboard interface.

### Timezone

Set your timezone for accurate timestamps in the dashboard.

### Email Notifications

Configure which emails you receive:

| Notification | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Usage Alerts** | When approaching quota limits |
| **Credit Expiration** | Before purchased credits expire |
| **Billing** | Payment confirmations and failures |
| **Team Updates** | When added to or removed from workspaces |

## Security

### Changing Password

For email/password accounts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Security">
    Click "Security" in account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Current Password">
    Verify your identity with your current password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Choose a strong new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Re-enter the new password and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Connected Accounts

If you signed up with Google or GitHub, your authentication is managed by that provider. To change your password, use your Google or GitHub account settings.

### Sessions

View and manage active sessions:

- See all devices where you're logged in
- Sign out of individual sessions
- Sign out of all sessions except current

## Your Workspaces

View all workspaces you're a member of:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Workspace** | Workspace name |
| **Role** | Your role (Owner, Editor, Viewer) |
| **Joined** | When you joined |

Click a workspace to switch to it.

## Leaving a Workspace

To leave a workspace you don't own:

1. Find the workspace in Your Workspaces
2. Click "Leave Workspace"
3. Confirm

<Warning>
  You cannot leave a workspace if you're the only owner. Transfer ownership first or delete the workspace.
</Warning>

## Deleting Your Account

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent. All your data, including workspaces you own, will be deleted.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Account** → **Delete Account**
2. Enter your password to confirm
3. Acknowledge the consequences
4. Click "Delete Account"

Before deleting:
- Transfer ownership of any workspaces you want to preserve
- Download any files you need to keep
- Export templates you want to save
